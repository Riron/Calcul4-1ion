Tutorial

  • Welcome !

    Calcula+ion is a nice and funny game in which you can challenge your friends at mental calculation. Solve as many operations as possible in 1 minute and become the best at calculation !

  • How to play #1

    Start the game by answering the first operation.

  • How to play #2

    Just answer the question by taping on the pad. Your points are displayed on the top, as your previous current and next question are being displayed above the pad. Don't forget to check for the timer !

  • Rules

    Score 5 points for a good answer, 0 for a pass, and -3 for a mistake. A party is made of 3 rounds, and each round last 1 minute.
